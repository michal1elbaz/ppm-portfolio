LegalZoom

LegalZoom is the leader in online legal document and forms. While at LegalZoom, I worked on the mobile website, as well as building out a future platform using responsive design based on Rails.

Shopsy

Shopsy is a fashion search engine that combined outfits and remixed them by color, style, and body types. I was a co-founder of Shopsy, and we were a part of the Techstars Boston Winter 2012 class.

Reflect

Reflect is a P&G subsidiary company that focused on customized beauty care products that people can order online. I was responsible for visual design, email campaign, product photography and retouching.

Zenbe

Zenbe was a web based email client with focus on new ways to interact with email and file attachments. I worked on the early prototype of the app, as well as very early branding exercises.

FixYa

FixYa is a community based Q&A resource for fix and repair for all consumer products. I was responsible for UI design, prototypes, as well as assisting our engineering team on front end code.

MokaFive

MokaFive focused on making computer virtualization easy to deploy and use by individual users and small businesses. I worked on some visual design for their website and print collaterals.

Presto

The Presto Printing Mailbox was a device made to receive email through traditional medium. I designed the website, print collaterals, and was a part of a major rebranding effort.
