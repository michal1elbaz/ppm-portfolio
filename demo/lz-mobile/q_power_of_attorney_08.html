When will powers be effective?

With a power of attorney, you can choose to grant your agent the power to make decisions for you as soon as the document is properly signed, or only if you become incapacitated and are unable to make decisions on your own.

Mental incapacity means the inability to manage your financial affairs, as determined by the written certification of a physician. A power of attorney that is effective only upon mental incapacity is called a "springing" power of attorney.
