What is the name of your business?

In California, the business name must contain: "Limited Liability Company" , "LLC" or "L.L.C.".

Please note: If this name is unavailable in California, we will contact you to ask for an alternate name.

Will you be doing business under any other name?

California allows businesses to use multiple names. For example, a business formed with the name Smith & Jones LLC could also do business under the name “Super Steakhouse”. To learn more, click here.
