Stay compliant and avoid fines

Once a business is formed, there is almost always additional paperwork, permits, or forms required to be filed. If this paperwork is not filed on time, a business can be fined and penalized. We can help your business complete this paperwork.

California Statement of Information

Within 90 days of forming your LLC, California requires that you file a Statement of Information with the Secretary of State, or pay a $250 penalty.

Would you like LegalZoom to complete the Statement of Information and submit it directly to the Secretary of State? (An additional fee of $55 will apply.) To learn more, click here.

California Seller's Permit

Most businesses that sell goods in California are required to obtain a Seller's Permit from the California State Board of Equalization.
