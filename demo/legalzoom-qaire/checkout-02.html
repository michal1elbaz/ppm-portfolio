Personalized Operating Agreement

Personalized Operating Agreement

Every LLC should adopt a personalized operating agreement to govern its operations. We provide your business with a personalized, state-specific operating agreement that includes important provisions covering:

Company Record-Keeping and Formalities
Clearly states that regular meetings of managers or members are NOT required

Ownership and Profit Sharing
Governs how profits are distributed to the members and provides the method for admitting new members into the LLC

Withdrawal and Dissolution
Provides a method for how members may withdraw and how the company may be dissolved

Restrictions on Transfer
This provision in the Operating Agreement specifies when and how a member of the LLC is allowed to sell his or her interest to a third party. During our online questionnaire, you can decide whether or not to include any transfer restrictions.

Bank Account Provision
Authorizes the company to open a business bank account. Many banks require that the company adopt such a provision before opening an account
Indemnification Clause

Indemnification Clause

This document allows the LLC to indemnify its members, managers and officers and cover legal expenses when they are acting on behalf of the company.
