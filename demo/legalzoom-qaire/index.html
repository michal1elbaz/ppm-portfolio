The majority of our customers form their LLC in the state where they plan to locate and run their business. However, the following types of businesses often choose to form their business in Delaware:

  • Businesses seeking investments from venture capital groups
  • Businesses with plans for an initial public offering (IPO) in the next few years

If you form your business in a state other than where it will be located, your business will most likely have to file additional paperwork and pay additional fees to "foreign qualify" in the state where the business is located.

If you have more questions about business formation or foreign qualification, please call us at (855) 773-0880.
